Orders & Purchasing

Everything about placing and managing your orders

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is simple! Browse our collection, select your items and sizes, add them to your bag, and click "Order via WhatsApp". You'll be redirected to WhatsApp where you can confirm your order with our team. We'll respond within minutes during business hours.

How will I know my order is confirmed?

Once you send your order via WhatsApp, our team will reply with an order confirmation including:

  • Order summary with items and sizes
  • Total amount in Naira
  • Payment details
  • Estimated delivery timeline

Keep this confirmation for your records.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes! You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it, as long as it hasn't been shipped yet. Simply message us on WhatsApp with your order details and requested changes. After 2 hours, modifications may not be possible if the item is already being prepared for shipment.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No minimum order! You can purchase a single item or multiple pieces. However, orders above 200,000 qualify for free express shipping within Nigeria.

What if an item is out of stock?

If an item is out of stock, we'll notify you immediately on WhatsApp. You can choose to:

  • Wait for restock (we'll notify you when available)
  • Choose an alternative size or color
  • Select a different product
  • Cancel that item from your order

Shipping & Delivery

Delivery times, costs, and tracking information

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs depend on your location:

  • Lagos: ₦2,000 - ₦3,500 (24-48 hours)
  • Other Nigerian cities: ₦3,500 - ₦5,500 (2-5 days)
  • International: Calculated based on destination and weight
  • Free shipping: Orders above ₦200,000 within Nigeria

Exact shipping cost will be confirmed when you place your order.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery timelines vary by location:

  • Lagos: 24-48 hours
  • Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan: 2-3 days
  • Other Nigerian states: 3-5 days
  • International: 7-14 business days

Express delivery is available for Lagos orders at an additional cost.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, we'll send you a tracking number via WhatsApp. You can use this number to track your package in real-time. We also provide updates at each stage of delivery.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Popular destinations include:

  • USA/Canada: 7-10 days
  • UK/Europe: 7-12 days
  • Ghana/South Africa: 5-7 days
  • UAE/Middle East: 5-8 days

Contact us on WhatsApp for a shipping quote to your specific location.

What if I don't receive my package?

If your package hasn't arrived within the estimated delivery window, contact us immediately on WhatsApp. We'll investigate with our shipping partner and either reship your order or provide a full refund. We stand behind every delivery.

Payments

Payment methods, security, and billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Bank Transfer (All Nigerian banks)
  • POS Payment (for Lagos deliveries)
  • Cash on Delivery (Lagos only, with 30% deposit)
  • PayPal (International orders)
  • Wise/TransferWise (International orders)
  • Crypto (USDT, BTC — on request)

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely! All transactions are secure. Bank transfers go directly to our verified business account. For international orders, we use PayPal's buyer protection. We never ask for your card details via WhatsApp. If anyone claiming to be from THEEBLACK•LUX asks for sensitive information, please report it to us immediately.

What currency do you charge in?

Our prices are displayed in Nigerian Naira (₦). For international customers, we can provide quotes in USD, GBP, or EUR based on the current exchange rate. Payment in foreign currency is accepted via PayPal or Wise.

Do you offer installment payments?

For orders above ₦500,000, we offer flexible payment plans:

  • 50/50 Plan: 50% upfront, 50% before shipping
  • 3-Part Plan: 40% upfront, 30% mid-way, 30% before shipping

Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss payment plan options for your order.

What if there's a payment issue?

If you experience any payment issues (failed transfer, double charge, etc.), contact us immediately on WhatsApp with proof of payment. We'll resolve the issue within 24 hours. For bank transfer issues, we work directly with our bank to trace and resolve problems quickly.

Returns & Exchanges

Our return policy and exchange process

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • With all tags attached
  • In original packaging
  • Not worn, washed, or altered

Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless defective.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes! Size exchanges are free within 7 days of delivery. Simply contact us on WhatsApp with:

  • Your order number
  • Current size
  • Desired size

We'll arrange pickup of the original item and ship the new size. Subject to availability.

How long do refunds take?

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within:

  • Bank Transfer: 24-48 hours
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Store Credit: Immediate

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the item was defective or incorrect.

What if I receive a damaged or wrong item?

We're sorry if this happens! Contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photos of the issue. We'll:

  • Send a replacement immediately (free shipping)
  • Or provide a full refund including shipping

We take quality seriously and will make it right.

How we ensure every product is 100% genuine

Are your products 100% authentic?

Sizing & Fit

Find your perfect fit with our sizing guides

Where can I find the size guide?

We have a comprehensive size guide covering USA, UK, EU, and Nigerian sizing for all categories. View our complete Size Guide →

What if I'm between sizes?

General recommendations:

  • Sneakers: Size up if you have wide feet
  • Hoodies/Tees: Size up for oversized fit, down for fitted
  • Jeans: Stick to your usual waist size
  • Suits: Always size up — easier to tailor down

When in doubt, message us on WhatsApp with your measurements and we'll recommend the best size!

Do sneakers run true to size?

It depends on the brand and model:

  • Nike Jordans: Generally true to size
  • Nike Dunks: True to size
  • Adidas Superstar: Run slightly large — consider half size down
  • Yeezy: Run small — size up half size

Each product listing includes specific fit notes. Check the description before ordering!

How do I measure myself?

For accurate measurements:

  • Use a soft measuring tape
  • Wear fitted clothing (not bulky)
  • Stand naturally — don't pull tape tight
  • Have a friend help if possible

See our Size Guide for detailed measurement instructions for chest, waist, hips, feet, and wrists.
